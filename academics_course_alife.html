Artificial Life

What would happen if we took inspiration from Darwin's theory of Survival of the Fittest and applied it to robot design?
This is exactly what I was exploring in the Artificial Life (ALife) course.

The field of artificial life was revolutionized by Karl Sims and the creation of his evolved virtual creatures. Much like Karl Sims' work, the coursework throughout ALife was focused on the process of creating and optimizing simulated creatures in a virtual physics environment.

For more details on my course project, and how you also could do a similar project, check out my project gitHub Repo. In the repo you will find plenty of information on the genetic encoding of my virtual creatures, how the virtual bodies of the creatures are built, controlled, and evolved, and videos of the best results of the project.
